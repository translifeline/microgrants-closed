Microgrants offers funding for:

  • Court order name changes
  • Passports
  • Drivers licenses or state IDs
  • Tribal IDs
  • Immigration documents

At this time we are not able to offer assistance for:

  • Birth certificates
  • Housing
  • Medical costs

Please read our FAQ for questions you may have about applying for or qualifying for a microgrant. Trans & Nonbinary folks of ALL ages are welcome to apply!

Please note that the application typically closes in less than a minute. We know that the need is huge, and we are nowhere near reaching that need - wea re continuously working on ways to address this, while working within the confines of financial limitations.

75% of microgrants each cycle are reserved for trans people who are Black, Indigenous or a person of color. Our application will first ask about your race. You can learn more about our equity policy here.
