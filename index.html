Microgrants offers funding for court ordered name changes, passports, drivers license or state IDs, birth certificates, and Tribal IDS. At this time we are not able to offer assistance for housing, medical costs/HRT, non-US residents, and non-US documents.

Please read our FAQ for questions you may have about applying or qualifying for a microgrant.

75% of microgrants each cycle are reserved for trans people who identify as Black, Indigenous, or a person of color. Our application will first ask how you identify. You can learn more about our equity policy here.

We are working to make our application process more accessible to our community. If you have feedback on the application process, please let us know here!

What to do in the meantime:

  • Visit our resource library and get yourself familiar with the name change process
  • Call the Hotline for emotional support

Las micro-becas ofrecen financiación para los cambios de nombre ordenados por los tribunales, los pasaportes, los permisos de conducir o las identificaciones estatales, las identificaciones tribales y los certificados de nacimiento.
